About CityLink Ghana

Connecting people to trusted businesses across Ghana.

Who We Are

CityLink Ghana is a modern local business directory platform designed to help people discover trusted businesses, services, restaurants, hotels, salons, repair shops, schools and more across Ghana.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make it easy for people to find reliable local businesses while helping businesses grow their visibility online.
