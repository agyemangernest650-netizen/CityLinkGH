Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

01
Information We Collect

When you use CityLinkGH, we may collect:

  • Name, phone number and email when submitting a listing or booking
  • Business details including address, photos and operating hours
  • Payment transaction references (we do not store card details)
  • Usage data such as pages visited and search queries
02
How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Display your business, job or room listing on the platform
  • Send booking confirmations and reminders via SMS or WhatsApp
  • Process payments through Paystack
  • Improve the platform and fix technical issues
  • Contact you about your listing status
03
Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase (Firestore), which is hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure. Images are stored on Cloudinary's secure CDN. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

04
SMS Communications

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive SMS notifications related to your listings and bookings via Arkesel. You can opt out by contacting us at any time.

05
Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Paystack — payment processing
  • Google Firebase — database and authentication
  • Cloudinary — image storage
  • Arkesel — SMS delivery
06
Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time. Contact us at hello@citylinkgh.com and we will remove your information within 7 days.

07
Contact

For privacy concerns, email hello@citylinkgh.com or message us on WhatsApp.
